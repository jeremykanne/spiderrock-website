Intelligent Order Routing

Quantitative Approach

Routing That Learns from the Market

SpiderRock's routing algorithms use a quantitative approach to order routing, enabling you to actively participate in price discovery by leaning on real-time fair value calculations. Orders are routed to maximize execution quality based on volatility surfaces, alpha probabilities, and live market microstructure data.

Our machine learning framework continually monitors and takes advantage of market micro-dynamic shifts, allowing for precise order routing based on a user-defined level of aggression. The system supports multi-leg spread orders, limit orders, and dark quotes, with basket risk controls managing position limits across all orders simultaneously.

This systematizes and scales derivatives strategy execution to institutional capacity while maintaining the precision required for complex options strategies.

Order Handling

Multi-Mode Execution

Market Sweeping

Aggressively sweep across multiple exchanges simultaneously to fill orders quickly, leveraging SpiderRock's direct exchange connectivity and low-latency infrastructure.

Spread Orders

Route multi-leg spread orders to maximize fill rates while managing leg risk across execution venues. The system handles complex options spreads with real-time leg risk monitoring.

Limit Orders
