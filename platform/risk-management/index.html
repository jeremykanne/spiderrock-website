Real-Time Risk Management

Order and portfolio risk controls with comprehensive limit monitoring and automated safeguards at every level.

Order-Level Controls

Order Risk Management

Risk group limits and risk counters enforce controls at the order level. Every order is evaluated against pre-configured risk parameters before routing to any exchange or dark pool. This creates a robust pre-trade risk gate that prevents limit breaches before they occur.

Risk counters track cumulative order flow across all strategies and accounts in real time, enabling immediate automated responses when configurable thresholds are approached.

✓ Risk group limits
✓ Per-order risk counters
✓ Exchange-level controls
✓ Real-time pre-trade checks

Portfolio-Level Controls

Portfolio Risk Management

Risk record aggregation provides a consolidated view of position risk across all strategies, accounts, and underlying exposures. SpiderRock's advanced risk management tools monitor Greeks, delta exposure, and P&L in real time, giving traders and risk managers complete visibility into portfolio risk.

This enables firms to shape their portfolio dynamically based on changing market conditions while maintaining strict adherence to risk limits.

✓ Risk record aggregation
✓ Greeks monitoring (delta, gamma, vega, theta)
✓ P&L attribution
✓ Portfolio stress testing
