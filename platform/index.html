Built for How You Trade

Systematic Dispersion Arbitrage

Dispersion arbitrage strategies typically involve short index options positions paired with long exposure in individual constituents or related names. These strategies are actively managed to maintain near delta neutral profiles, with exposure balanced through dynamic hedging, options Greeks alignment, and scenario-based risk analysis.

Portfolio Managers can easily design, execute, and manage these strategies at scale. Through integrated APIs clients access live market data and analytics, monitor real-time risk, and deploy risk-constrained baskets of orders with embedded execution logic and automated hedging across large symbol sets.

Platform Features Docs

Systematic Long/Short Valuation Arbitrage

Long/Short valuation arbitrage strategies aim to maintain a balanced portfolio of single-name long or short volatility positions. These portfolios may span a wide range of underliers and can optionally include index or ETF exposure.

Strategies are typically constructed to capture relative value based on quantitative valuation models and are actively managed through dynamic hedging as underlying prices evolve. Through integrated APIs, clients access live market data and analytics, check real-time risk and insert risk-constrained basket of orders with embedded execution and automated hedging across large symbol sets.

Research / Special Situations Trading

Special situations trading is typically driven by discretionary teams with deeper sector expertise and proprietary research. These workflows are less systematic, with traders actively managing execution and risk through high-touch, GUI-based tools.

SpiderRock provides advanced trading interfaces, including options montages and real-time analytics, enabling traders to express views and manage risk in a highly interactive environment. Traders can incorporate proprietary valuation models ranging from full volatility surfaces to client-defined ATM inputs integrated with SpiderRock's implied volatility framework.

Order Management

SpiderRock's Platform allows clients to participate directly in price discovery through real-time model-driven estimates derived from continuously updated volatility surfaces and analytics. It combines quantitative models and machine learning-based signals to monitor market microstructure and optimize order routing. Clients can calibrate execution from passive to aggressive using these signals enabling precise, scalable deployment of derivative strategies.
