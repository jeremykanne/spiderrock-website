SpiderRock Partners with CPZAI to Deliver an AI-Native Systematic Options Trading Workflow

July 7, 2026

SpiderRock's Options Data and Analytics Now Available on CPZAI

CHICAGO, July 7, 2026 — SpiderRock, a provider of institutional level options analytics, historical data, and trading technology solutions, today announced a partnership with CPZAI, an AI-native systematic trading operating system designed for hedge funds, proprietary trading firms, and institutional investors.

The partnership brings SpiderRock's live and historical options analytics and market data to the CPZAI operating system, providing firms with access to institutional-grade options content within an AI-powered research and development environment.

Users can access SpiderRock's live implied volatility surfaces, Greeks, volatility analytics, and historical options datasets alongside CPZAI's AI-native research environment, systematic strategy development tools, back-testing capabilities, and governance framework.

Firms can use SpiderRock's options data and analytics in systematic trading and quantitative research workflows while maintaining consistency between live and historical analytics across strategy development, validation, and analysis. Linking these systems also expands the range of workflows available to firms already utilizing SpiderRock execution services.

About SpiderRock

SpiderRock Data & Analytics is a division of SpiderRock Technology Solutions, a provider of industry-leading options trading solutions. SpiderRock Data & Analytics is an exchange-licensed redistributor of market data, providing US and EU stocks and options market data in a raw and normalized format.

SpiderRock's proprietary live analytics offer a low-cost delivery of market data and options analytics without requiring clients to make a significant investment in infrastructure. In addition, SpiderRock's robust historical datasets updated daily from live markets are ideal for research, back testing, and making data-driven decisions.

About CPZAI

CPZAI is an AI-native systematic trading operating system that enables institutions to research, develop, test, deploy, and govern quantitative trading strategies. The operating system combines AI-native development tools, large-scale data integration, back testing, risk management, and execution workflows within a unified institutional environment.
