Overview

What is the Chart Tool?

The SpiderRock Chart Tool enables users to visualize options surfaces and compare relative volatilities between stocks by creating simple and more complex spreads or ratios. Analyze the impact of earnings and options skew on implied and historical volatility across stocks, ETFs, and indices using our multi-year database of point-in-time option surfaces.

Users can quickly develop, save, and share multiple charts to collaborate with their peers. The data is easily downloaded for further analysis.
