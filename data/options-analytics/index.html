Built for Speed

Real-Time Volatility Feed

SpiderRock volatility feeds are built on option analytics derived from our proprietary numerical pricing libraries and are based on discrete dividend estimates where appropriate. The feeds update on every OPRA tick (TICK), at fixed intervals (LOOP), and at the end of each trading session (CLOSE).

Leveraging our technology platform, SpiderRock sets the standard in reliable, high-performance, intelligent options analytics. We use a market-tested proprietary methodology to deliver superior speed, throughput, and accuracy to protect our clients' edge.

TICK

LOOP

Fixed interval updates

CLOSE

End of session

Sub-millisecond processing of analytics for low latency delivery. Arbitrage-free volatility surfaces tied together with no-arbitrage across strikes to support live trading operations.

Multiple Formats

Two Delivery Mechanisms

SpiderStream Multicast Feed

A low-latency analytics feed that adds options content to raw market feeds. Includes normalized OPRA and CME prices with a reduced bandwidth profile that is more manageable and cost-effective.

  • —ADAPTIVE — Implied vol NBBO quotes, surface prices, Greeks with synchronized underlying prices, forecasted dividends, and interest rates
  • —SECURE — Redundant infrastructure in US through NY4/5 or CH2 data centers, globally available via partner data ecosystems
  • —ARBITRAGE-FREE — Volatility surfaces designed for trading, tied together with no-arbitrage across strikes
  • —REAL-TIME — Sub-millisecond processing of analytics for low latency delivery

SpiderStream over MLink API

A powerful and effective API for Websocket or REST query access to live market data for all US listed equities, futures, and options — including full-scope options analytics and volatility surfaces.

  • —EASY TO CONSUME — Internet access or cross-connect in Equinix NY4 or CH2. Unlimited user API calls
  • —BROAD — Live and prior day records covering all listed US equity and futures derivative markets
  • —EFFICIENT — High throughput operations with low latency message delivery
  • —LIVE ANALYTICS — On-the-fly computation of implied volatilities and Greeks, plus minute bars and trade aggregations

Use Cases

How Clients Use Our Analytics

GUI & Trading Platform Integration

Handling full market data feeds to build displays for customer-facing applications, internal trading, or risk platforms is expensive. With SpiderStream over MLink, integrate via Websocket or REST and scale off SpiderRock's 100GB market data infrastructure backbone — for the cost of a market data feed.

Optimize Live Trading Strategies

Consume SpiderRock's feed with the most accurate volatility surfaces for US options available on the market. Significantly improve model accuracy and reduce CPU load by offloading analytics calculation — reducing hardware requirements without compromising data quality.

Risk & P&L Monitoring

Accurate surface prices allow clients to optimize deployed capital and better understand potential risks. Perform "what-if" analysis, real-time risk with margin, and overnight portfolio risk using messages like Implied Quote and Close Marks delivered over the multicast feed.

Interactive Database Querying

Query tables with live prices, quotes, and prints across all asset classes sourced from US exchanges — enhanced with live proprietary analytics calculated on every option tick and at regular intervals. Reliable, low-cost delivery without a significant infrastructure investment.
