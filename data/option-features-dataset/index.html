Which is a Better Predictor: Options or Stocks?

The truth lies in combining both. While traditional stock data reflects overall market performance, options data offers a forward-looking lens into market sentiment and volatility.

Metrics like delta pressure, implied volatility, and order flow analytics provide deeper insights into probable market movements.
