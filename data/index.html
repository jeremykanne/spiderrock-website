Our Data & Analytics

Three Product Tiers

Real-Time Data

SpiderRock delivers raw and normalized US-based equity, options, and futures data feeds via multicast channels. Our normalized feed provides a reduced bandwidth profile for better manageability and cost effectiveness, and our client-side APIs turn high multicast packet volumes into individual messages at low latencies.

Learn More →

Options Analytics

Our highly accurate and adaptive pricing methods cover all US-listed options and options on futures. By incorporating data from our API environment into our analytics framework, we offer a low-latency analytics feed providing options content including normalized OPRA and CME, NBBO implied volatility quotes, theoretical surface prices, Greeks, and trade prints.

Learn More →

Historical Data

SpiderRock has a suite of historical data products in multiple asset classes with the ability to deliver data via flat files and RESTful API. Our market data and analytics enable data-driven decisions by examining historical theoretical volatility surfaces, implied volatilities, skew slopes, Greeks, and stock, option, and futures price and print information.

How We Deliver Data

Volatility Charts

Intuitive web-based access to historical and implied volatility options data, including surfaces, volumes, and open interest. A powerful visualization tool for researching relative option values.

Live APIs

API access to multiple live market data feeds and a wide selection of analytics for single stocks, ETFs, options, and futures. SpiderStream over MLink provides seamless access to real-time and delayed institutional-grade data and options analytics.

Normalized Feeds

Specialized data feeds designed to simplify access to high-quality, low-latency data at reasonable costs. Our normalized feed reduces bandwidth while maintaining the data integrity required for institutional workflows.

Cloud-Based Delivery
