Data Quality

Rigorous Data Processing

Raw exchange data contains errors, anomalies, and inconsistencies. SpiderRock applies institutional-grade processing to deliver clean, research-ready data.

  • 1
    Corporate Action Adjustments

    Prices and volumes are adjusted for stock splits, reverse splits, spin-offs, and special dividends — ensuring continuity across the full history.

  • 2
    Outlier Detection & Cleansing

    Erroneous prints and quotes are flagged and removed. Obvious bad ticks are corrected, and suspicious data is annotated rather than silently discarded.

  • 3
    Symbol Mapping & Continuity

    Ticker changes, mergers, delistings, and re-listings are tracked so you can follow securities through corporate events without losing historical context.
