Overview

What is VOL2G?

VOL2G is SpiderRock's volatility data bundle featuring implied at-the-money volatilities and skew slopes for fixed-term option expirations at multiple points ranging from 5 to 504 days.

The dataset includes bid/ask option pricing, synchronized underlying prices, earnings dates, earnings-driven volatility, and complete reference data — everything needed for high-quality equity volatility research.

VOLG2 users receive a complete set of reference data, including security IDs, asset definitions, and corporate action adjustments needed to value and backtest options portfolios.

Why VOL2G

Extending Your Research Horizon

VOL2G datasets are excellent for analyzing relative movements in volatility across assets over longer time scales (days to years). By removing the complexity of individual expiring strikes, VOL2G supports the study of volatility strategies at fixed maturity points.

This makes VOL2G perfect for using implied volatility data in conjunction with stock data and other alternative datasets — credit card purchases, social media data, weather, and other event-driven volatility for individual companies or groups of assets.

Flexible Delivery

Multiple Formats and Access Methods

Cloud File Delivery

Daily subscription files stored in cloud-based storage, partitioned by date. Available for daily downloads or bulk historical access.

Liberator API

On-demand data access by ticker or group and date/time ranges via Python, R, C++, C#, JavaScript, and Excel plug-in.

Intraday Add-Ons

Enhance VOL2G with intraday option surfaces and market price snapshots as frequent as 5 minutes. Daily prints with analytics, and minute bar data.
