Market Data Partners

SpiderRock data powers a growing ecosystem of financial analytics platforms and research tools.

SpiderRock's options data and analytics powers a curated network of partner platforms, giving clients access to complementary analytical tools built on SpiderRock's institutional-grade data. These partnerships extend the reach of SpiderRock's market data into specialized research, machine learning, and independent price verification workflows.

Our Network

Data Partners

NewMark Risk

Analytics

NewMark Risk is a fin-tech company focused on providing option-derived analytics for institutional investors. Leveraging SpiderRock's high-quality data, NewMark Risk delivers alternative data and options-derived analytics with unmatched accuracy and quality — providing insights not currently available anywhere else in the market.

NewMark Risk has garnered experience across the financial and technology sectors where data requires significant initial work to scrub and prepare prior to drawing meaningful insights.

Canari

Machine Learning

Canari.dev is a web-based platform bringing the power of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence to options traders. Using AI to track patterns in screen trades, cross underlying volatility spreads, and term structure shapes, Canari's goal is to reinvent the workflow for volatility trades that don't belong in the order book.

Canari makes volatility trading cheaper and more transparent by leveraging SpiderRock's comprehensive options data ecosystem.

Skylight IPV

Price Verification

Skylight IPV is an independent price verification company combining collective experience and knowledge with innovative technology. Skylight provides risk managers and product controllers at market-making institutions with extensive, reliable, and independent OTC derivatives valuation data.

Using SpiderRock data, Skylight delivers more effective and efficient solutions for accounting and risk functions at institutional trading desks.

Exponential

Data Technology

Exponential develops bespoke data analytics, signals, and global macro forecasts for institutional investors. With over 25 years of experience in institutional investment and high-frequency market-making, Exponential features the Unifier™ high-performance hybrid data warehouse and API.

Unifier empowers enterprises to innovate more rapidly by combining on-prem and cloud data technology with tightly integrated Signals and generative-AI-powered analytics.

Nations Indexes

Volatility Indexes

Nations Indexes is the world's leading independent developer of volatility and option-enhanced indexes. By deconstructing option skew and utilizing rigorous methodologies, Nations Indexes creates innovative solutions that help investors and traders make more data-driven decisions.

Their indexes provide deeper insight into option prices, allowing traders and investors to quantify what has been subjective and trade with knowledge, not opinion.
