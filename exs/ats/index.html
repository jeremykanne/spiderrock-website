How the SpiderRock ATS Works

Electronic Auction Innovation

Two Auction Mechanisms

Our options ATS offers two different auction mechanisms which allow clients to interact with off-screen liquidity.

Block Auction

This innovative auction process removes manual interaction and invites institutional clients to transact electronically with a wide array of liquidity providers via the SpiderRock ATS. Initiating clients can access both the SpiderRock Network of liquidity providers as well as existing trading counterparties upon request.

Auction Duration 15 – 300 seconds
Counterparties Existing + SR Network
Identity Can reveal to counterparties
Best Price Offset Yes
Limit Price Update Anytime during auction
Block Auctions Docs

Flash Auction

This instantaneous auction attempts to match an initiating order with auto-responding liquidity. Our two-step process requires liquidity providers to respond with price and size before the initiating order price is used to match. In all circumstances, auction responses are revealed, providing valuable price discovery.

Auction Duration Immediate
Counterparties Auto-responders
Identity Anonymous only
Best Price Offset Responders only
Limit Price Update Not applicable
