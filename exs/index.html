FINRA-Regulated Market Access

Regulated Electronic Execution

SpiderRock EXS LLC is a FINRA-regulated broker dealer and NFA member specializing in electronic execution and market access services. EXS can facilitate execution of all U.S.-listed equities, equity options, and futures via a low-latency infrastructure directly connected to exchanges or other providers' DMA systems.

EXS provides regulatory oversight for clients' trading activity, which includes provision of market access routes and risk controls, retention of all trading records, reporting, and surveillance obligations.

What EXS Provides

Comprehensive Market Access

Direct Market Access (DMA)

As a FINRA member, EXS can structure client-specific market access routes to optimize latency, minimize cost, preserve existing relationships, or provide redundancy. Clients can leverage their prime broker's infrastructure or use our direct connectivity to exchanges.

Pre-Trade Risk Layer

EXS provides a fully compliant 15c3-5 risk layer, allowing market access risk controls to check parent order validation, client firm risk exposure, and account authorization checks before any order reaches the market.

Expert Support Team

U.S.- and EU-based customer support specialists continually monitor the platform and respond to any client inquiries throughout the trading session. Dedicated coverage when it matters most.

Regulatory Oversight

EXS provides regulatory oversight including provision of market access routes and risk controls, retention of all trading records, reporting, and surveillance obligations.

Low-Latency Infrastructure

Execute all U.S.-listed equities, options, and futures via a low-latency infrastructure directly connected to exchanges. Optimized routing designed for institutional options workflows and large money allocations.

Platform Access

EXS clients gain access to the SpiderRock Platform, a high-performance cloud-based trading and risk system offering best-in-class algorithmic routing logic, real-time analytics, and integrated risk management.

Alternative Trading System

SpiderRock ATS

The SpiderRock ATS is an electronic trading network where initiating interest is efficiently matched with a broad and diverse pool of liquidity via a competitive and open auction process. Block and Flash auction mechanisms provide off-screen access for institutional participants.
